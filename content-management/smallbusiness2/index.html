About Us

J.R Clarke's has come a long way since it's first shop in Drury Lane in 1945, it was established as a partnership with other local grocery stores, when John R. Clarke and his wife Mary Ann opened a shop at 185 Drury Lane in Holborn, London. It started as a retailer of fresh foods and later expanded into packaged groceries such and organic produce. His trading philosophy still remains, as stated on a sign outside his first shop in Islington, was: "Your neighbourhood health foods store". Today it is run by his grandchildren and it's popularity and quality still remain as always.
